What is ConnectX ??
Here in ConnectX, we basically connect people who need some essential thing with the person who can supply them what we do is, we provide the needer and supplier with each others contact details such that they can get in contact with each other
When you visit our website, You have two options either you can supply or you can ask something that you need once you click either of the option, we ask you about what essential thing you need, For now we have limited the option as to standardise the website for pandemic situation like we are giving the option for Oxygen ,Covid bed Availablity, plasma and blood donation. Such that people can get the essential things as early as possible
Once you have done that, Then you will be taken to the detail page where you can fill the form, and add all your contact details, when you submit this form, you are ridirected to the page, and you are matched with the needer or supplier, with your essential item and you both,are given details about each other, and then you can go forward and contact them and get or give the essential item
